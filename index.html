How We Work With You

The name GRAFFT was derived from the concept of implanting a high performing entity into another body for improved performance. This serves as a metaphor for how we work with teams like yours to develop a hybrid of best practices that fit your needs and help you excel. We like to work in small dedicated teams along side your developers to improve code delivery, quality, and scalability in production. We use tools like Jira, Trello, Slack, and GitHub.

GRAFFT offers industry experience first and software development services second. We are here to help your full time employees succeed and to help you deliver your product.
