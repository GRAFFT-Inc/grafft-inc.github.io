How We Work With You

The name GRAFFT was derived from the concept of implanting a high performing entity into another body for maximized performance. This serves as a metaphor for how we work with teams like yours to develop a hybrid of best practices that fit your needs and help your teams perform in a way that is unique to them. We like to work along side your scrum teams to improve product value, code delivery, quality, and scalability in production. We use tools like Jira, Trello, Slack, and GitHub.

GRAFFT prioritizes industry experience first and software development services second. We are not merely augmented laborers, but are here to help your full time employees succeed and deliver your product.
