How we work with you

We like to work in small, dedicated teams built to fit your needs. Your team will be focused exclusively on your project. We work closely with you, keeping in constant communication to deliver your vision.

  • Some of our favorite communication tools are Trello, Slack, GitHub, Appear.in and Zeplin.
  • We write code with single-responsibility to minimize technical debt, make the code easier to test, and allow for easy feature changes.
  • We follow our favorite workflows defined in the GRAFFT Playbook.
