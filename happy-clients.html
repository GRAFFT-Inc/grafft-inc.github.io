Rejoiner

Rejoiner is an ecommerce revenue recovery SaaS application that empowers online retailers to reconnect with customers who have abandoned shopping carts filled with potential revenue. Businesses can set up automated email campaigns with activity and revenue triggers to reach out to customers who have abandoned their shopping carts and incentivize them to return and complete their purchase.

Bestest

Bestest is a social media platform and ad tech company that lets individuals, groups and organizations fansource (or friendsource) market data, feedback and opinions in real time.

Described by others as "Instagram meets SurveyMonkey," Bestest is emerging as the social feed for feedback.

We provide an alternative to clunky forms, impersonal surveys, expensive focus groups and inadequate social media tools by letting people and organizations poll anyone, anywhere, anytime they want--using picture, text and video polls that are social, simple and smart.
